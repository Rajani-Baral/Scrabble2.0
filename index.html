Rules

In "Anagrabble", your goal is to solve as many anagrams from the given letters as possible in the time limit of 90 seconds. The longer the anagram you solve, the more points you get for it, so play with this in mind. Have fun!


  • 4-letter words give:  x points
  • 5-letter words give:  y points
  • 6-letter words give:  z points
